MEETING AND EVENTS

 
The fabulous Fifty Five with its massive picture windows and outside terrace, 
effortlessly blends visionary design with exceptional dining & drinking. 
Whether it’s a lazy lunch or brunch, a divine afternoon tea or an enchanting 
dinner you’ll discover seasonal, handcrafted dishes, using fresh, locally sourced 
ingredients that are perfectly presented. 
						
 
The Fifty Five offers a magnetic setting for any occasion 
:- exceptional flavour combinations,innovative cocktail creations 
and an unrivalled collection of wines & champagnes,with service 
from our friendly and enthusiastic team that’s second to none.
The ambience of the Fifty Five evolves throughout the day and
at night the tempo rises to deliver a vibrant,  all-encompassing
entertainment experience with renowned house DJ’s in residence 
every Friday & Saturday.
						
 
At the Royal you’ll discover a collection of striking rooms & suites that imaginatively 
blend Victorian elegance with contemporary style. Decorated in soothing, soft tones 
and cleverly combining naturally inspired & modern materials, our guestrooms feature 
ingenious design touches with thoughtful luxury. PLEASE NOTE THAT WE ARE 
UNDERTAKING A PHASED REFURBISHMENT OF ALL BEDROOMS & SUITES.

Wedding 

 
More than any other occasion a wedding has to be flawless and 
that’s what we deliver at the Royal. Whether you dream of 
an intimate occasion with close family and friends or a grand celebration,
 at the Royal our beautiful wedding suites offer flexible spaces 
 that are just perfect with spectacular interiors that effortlessly 
 blend period charm with designer chic and glamour 
 throughout.

Meetings & Events 

 
With a collection of boutique private event spaces 
The Royal lends a prestigious context to all types of meetings &
conferences.Cutting edge design & high end style are
effortlessly blended with the distinguished grand proportions & 
period details of the building and our flexible rooms suit 
both small & large meetings.
