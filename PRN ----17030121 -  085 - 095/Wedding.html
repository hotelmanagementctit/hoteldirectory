Your Special Day 

 
More than any other occasion a wedding has to be flawless 
and that’s what we deliver at the Royal. Whether you
dream of an intimate occasion with close family and friends
or a grand celebration, at the Royal our beautiful wedding suites
offer flexible spaces that effortlessly blend period charm 
with designer chic and glamour throughout.
 Tying the knot at the Royal isn’t just about a superb 
setting and imaginative, carefully crafted food. Every wedding 
is special for our team and they’ll take care with every last detail 
to make sure it all goes smoothly from the planning to the day itself.

Your Ceremony 

 
The gorgeous Bruce & Wallace Suites on the first floor,
with sophisticated interiors and designer finishes, 
are beautiful venues for intimate wedding services.
 Alternatively, if you are planning to have all your friends 
and family in attendance to witness your nuptials, 
our fabulously stylish Ballroom is the perfect setting for 
larger ceremonies.And, if you dream of getting married 
in church, the magnificent Bridge of Allan Parish
Church and St Saviour’s Episcopal Church are 
beautiful venues and just two of the options located 
only a stones-throw from the hotel.

Service 

 
At the Royal you are assured of the highest calibre of service,
spectacular wedding suites and beautifully crafted food 
and drink, providing you with the flexibility to 
make the wedding celebration you have always 
dreamed of a reality. You will also be assigned a dedicated and 
experienced wedding executive who will help you plan and 
co-ordinate every aspect of your wedding celebrations and 
advise on those extra special touches that 
will make your big day even more perfect.
