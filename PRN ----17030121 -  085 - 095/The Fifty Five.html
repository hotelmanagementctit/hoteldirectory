Drinks 

 
The Fifty Five's signature is ‘gin’ & featuring over 50 
different varieties you’ll discover a selection that transcend
s other venues. Other tastes are also generously catered
 forwith an enviable list of wine & Champagnes as well as
 an eclectic array of cocktails. The ambience evolves
 throughout the day and at night The Fifty Five comes to
 life, and the tempo rises to deliver a vibrant, 
 all-encompassing entertainment experience with renowned
 house DJ’s in residence every Friday & Saturday.

Cocktails 

 
At The Fifty Five we are a little obsessed about our cocktails 
but we don’t take ourselves too seriously - just check out our
 eclectic selection of cocktails. Along with all the classics
 you’d expect, there’s a fabulous array of original creations.
 Grab one of the stools at the bar and you’ll be simply mesmerised
 by the theatre and performance of our crew as they create an
 exceptional cocktail just for you!! And if you don't see your 
 favourite cocktail, just ask a member of the team.
