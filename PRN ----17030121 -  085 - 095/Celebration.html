The BallRoom 

 
The stunning Ballroom offers a majestic and stylish space for 
an incredible black tie dinner, a grand ball or even a fashionable 
catwalk show. Totally restyled to offer unsurpassed luxury, the Ballroom
imaginatively fuses the elegant Victorian heritage, with contemporary 
designer chic. This extraordinary space also benefits from huge picture windows 
ensuring that theroom is flooded with natural light. The Ballroom also features a 
stylish private reception area providing the ultimate space for arrival drinks or 
cocktail receptions.

Smaller Than Event 

 
If you are planning a smaller event or celebration then the beautiful 
Bruce & Wallace Suites, offer an exclusive and equally impressive 
setting with signature panelling, gorgeous furnishings 
and an abundance of designer touches. Located on the first floor these 
exquisite rooms offer a remarkable backdrop for your occasion with the ultimate 
privacy. Supported by personal and impeccable service, luminous guest 
bedrooms, extravagantly beautiful interiors, and an array of 
stunning menus The Royal creates an unforgettable experience & 
backdrop for any occasion.

Your Event 

 
At the Royal we understand that catering for the tastes of you 
and your guests is essential and our acclaimed Executive
Chef & kitchen brigade simply excel in the food arena. 
Whether you’re looking for a magnificent formal dinner or, perhaps an indulgent
 and expansive buffet, at the Royal we offer a huge array of imaginative 
banqueting options or, alternatively why not meet our chef and create a bespoke menu
 that suits your requirements.
