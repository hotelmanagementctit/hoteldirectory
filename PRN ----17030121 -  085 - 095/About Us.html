THE ICONIC ROYAL IS REBORN 

The iconic Royal Hotel, sitting proudly in the heart of Bridge of Allan has been transformed with a massive, multi-million pound investment. 
The Royal has been sensationally reinvigorated & reborn, reflecting the essence of the original building, with its Victorian heritage & glamour, 
integrated with an abundance of alluring modern twists and sophisticated, bespoke design.

Seductive and striking the focal point of the new Royal Hotel is the fabulous Fifty Five Bar & Grill which dominates the ground 
floor & features massive picture windows onto the High Street. Blending visionary design with an exceptional menu The Fifty Five 
serves inspired cuisine to deliver an unforgettable dining experience, as well as featuring a range of over 50 gins that far surpasses other venues. 
The ambience evolves throughout the day and, as the light fades, The Fifty Five comes to life,to deliver a vibrant, all-encompassing entertainment 
experience with renowned house DJ’s in residence every Friday & Saturday. 

This ‘show-stopper’ of a venue is perfectly complemented by a collection of indulgent guestrooms that effortlessly blend cutting-edge design 
and functionality.  The visionary design also extends to the fabulous ballroom and two new private dining spaces where the elegant period features 
& generous proportions, have been married with striking bespoke chandeliers, designer fabrics & floor coverings and specially commissioned artwork.
With spectacular interiors and matched by a class-leading quality of service The Royal is the ultimate venue for any special occasion or celebration.

Featuring a veritable symphony of designer chic & glamour throughout, the Royal is bringing a bold new energy to Bridge of Allan, blending boutique 
style and sophistication. With distinctive design evident throughout every area, the Royal is once again setting a new benchmark of opulence & modernity
 for a stylish clientele.
