Meetings & Events 

 
From a high-powered board room strategy meeting or conference for
the entire company to an inspiring incentive event or, perhaps, 
a corporate seminar, at The Royal we deliver extraordinary 
experiences that exceed the expectations of both our clients 
and their guests. In this venue, that effortlessly blends cutting edge
 design & high end style with distinguished grand proportions and
period details, you’ll also discover the clever & subtle incorporation of 
ultra-modern technology ensuring the kind of flexibility that organisers 
dream of.

Packages 

 
With a collection of boutique private event spaces
 The Royal lends a prestigious context to all types of
 meetings & special occasions. With superlative surroundings,
 matched with impeccable service & sublime dining options, 
The Royal really is the perfect choice for your meeting and 
we offer an array of day delegate & 24 hour packages.

Meeting & suites 

 
The stunning Ballroom offers a stylish space for conferences 
and events or seminars with theatre style seating. 
Imaginatively fusing the elegant Victorian heritage with 
contemporary designer chic, massive picture windows also 
ensure that this impressive space is illuminated with 
natural light. For smaller meetings or events the Bruce & 
Wallace Suites, offer an exclusive and equally impressive
 setting and, located on the first floor, also offer the ultimate
 privacy. And it goes without saying that our rooms are 
served by the latest sophisticated lighting and technology, including a 
professional sound system and wireless internet.
